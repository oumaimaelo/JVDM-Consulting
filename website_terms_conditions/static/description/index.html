Website Terms and Conditions

Add some terms and conditions on your ecommerce and made it mandatory for your customers to read and accept them before confirming an order.

Salient Features

  • Provides the feature to the seller for setting Terms and Conditions for website shop.
  • Display terms and conditions without accepting them on checkout page.
  • Restricts the payment and pops a message if terms and conditions are not accepted.
  • Display the terms and conditions in two ways. On the same page and On a Pop Up window.
  • Configure the Terms & Conditions by your choice.
